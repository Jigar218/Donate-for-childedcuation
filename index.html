Vision

To realise a world where every child has access to quality values-based and holistic education.

Mission

Our mission is to mobilize and motivate non-school going children, between the ages of 6 and 14, from economically and socially backward rural areas of India, and put them firmly on the track of literacy through quality primary education. IMPACT is an initiative of alumni of the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, India. To serve as a global platform for like-minded individuals and institutions to participate in giving every child access to quality values-based and holistic education.
